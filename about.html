Dr.Vamsi Krishnaa

Orthopaedic & Joint Replacement Surgeon, MBBS.,DNB (ORTHO).,MNAMS., FIAS.,FIJR(Australia & France)

He further honed his skills after being selected as an Fellow at Sydney Orthopedic Research Institute, sydney, Australia from May 2018. Here, Dr. Vamsi was entrusted with the Adult Reconstruction Surgery services comprising Arthroscopy and Joint Replacement (Navigation & Robotic). Dr.vamsi also selected as Fellow at Lyon school of knee surgery, France and Concordia Hospital ,Rome, Italy. After returning to India, he began working as a consultant in Trauma, arthritis, sports medicine, arthroscopy and joint replacement with the two hospitals CHIRANJEEVI HOSPITALS –Jangareddygudem and Hyderabad. He is also Visiting Consultant in Meridian Hospital, Chennai. With his expert hands, healing touch and skill that have been honed by years of experience, Dr. Vamsi now conducts specialty outpatient clinics. Surgery and the improvement it brings to his patients has always attracted him, and so Dr.Vamsi routinely performs complex surgeries and operates daily at hospitals conducting Basic & Complex Trauma cases, major surgeries with Total Knee Replacement (TKR), Revision TKR, Total Hip Replacement (THR), Arthroscopic knee and shoulder reconstruction
